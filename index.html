Parm

Hey there! My name is Parmanand Sahadeo (or Parm for short). I am a Computer Engineering graduate from the City College of New York. I have studied Electrical and Robotics Engineering at Thomas A. Edison Career and Technical Education High School (GO TEAM 640!!). I learned about electronics and circuit design and my passion for technology began to develop. I followed this passion through to college, where I focused my studies on computer engineering.

In college, my passion for learning about computer hardware grew intensely. I was exposed to programming but it didn't spark my interest until my senior year. In my senior year of college, I worked on my senior design project with a few peers to create our version of a "Smart Cane".

While I worked on testing the ultrasonic sensor, my passion for coding developed. I felt more confident as a programmer; being able to code and learn at my own pace. After graduation, I decided to pursue a new path: the path towards becoming a sofware engineer.

After spending some time deciding where to pursue my new career choice, a colleague showed me a flier for the Flatiron School. I enrolled in the highly selective and highly competitive Web Development Fellowship. After being accepted, I spent the next five months learning all about being a Full Stack Web Developer. I learned to code in Ruby and build websites using Rails and Sinatra frameworks. I learned about database management using SQL and front end design using HTML/CSS and JavaScript.

It was a great chapter in my life. I learned the skills and resources needed to become a great programmer. Now, I am finally confident in a career choice as a Software Engineer. I am very excited to begin my career!
